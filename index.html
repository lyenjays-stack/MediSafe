The problem

Taking medicine correctly is harder than it looks

Especially for elderly people managing several prescriptions, or families caring from a distance.

01

Easy to forget

Busy days and similar-looking pills make missed or doubled doses common — and sometimes dangerous.

02

Wrong pill, wrong time

Without a check, it's easy to take the afternoon tablet in the morning, or grab the wrong bottle entirely.

03

Family can't see

Children living away have no simple way to know whether mum or dad actually took today's medicine.

The app

Five simple screens, one calm routine

Designed to be gentle for patients and reassuring for the family who care about them.

Screen 01

Today

The day's doses at a glance, with a progress ring and a clear "take now" for the next one due.

Screen 02

AI pill verification

Point the camera at the pill — MediSafe confirms it's the right one from the right compartment before you take it.

Screen 03

History

A weekly adherence chart and a clear log of every dose taken or missed, so progress is easy to see.

Screen 04

Caregiver

Family see a live status, get missed-dose alerts, and can call or message in one tap.

Screen 05

Add medication

Set up a new medicine in seconds — name, dose, times of day, and which of the seven box compartments it lives in.

The hardware

A box that lights the way

MediBox is a seven-compartment smart dispenser. When a dose is due, the right compartment glows and the app sends a gentle reminder — pairing the physical box with the digital app.

Lit compartments

The correct slot lights up at dose time, so there's no guessing.

Connected to the app

An ESP32 chip syncs the box with MediSafe over Wi-Fi for live status and history.

AI-checked before you swallow

The camera verifies the pill matches the compartment, adding a safety net for every dose.

How it works

From reminder to peace of mind

1
